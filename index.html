Welcome to our restaurant

Our restaurant is a Thai restaurant in New Zealand that focuses on offering authentic, high-quality Thai cuisine. With a diverse and well-known menu, we use fresh ingredients and genuine spices from Thailand to maintain the true flavors of Thai food. Besides providing delicious dishes, we pay close attention to every step of preparation and service to ensure that our customers have the best possible experience.
